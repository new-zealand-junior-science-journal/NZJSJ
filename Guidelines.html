Guidelines

The New Zealand Junior Science Journal invites submissions of Core Concept papers or Independent Investigation reports with a length of two to five pages (excluding references). Submissions are accepted across two age divisions: Years 7–8 and Years 9–10.

All reports must be prepared using the appropriate template linked below. Authors are encouraged to read the Writing Reports Guide before beginning. Figures should appear within the paper and also be uploaded separately with captions.

For the 2026–2027 Spring edition, submissions must be received by May 31st, 2026. The submission portal opens on March 1st, 2026.

Formatting Specifications

  • Length: 2–5 pages (references not included)
  • Font: Times New Roman
  • Font size: 10 pt
  • Layout: Official NZJSJ templates only
  • Margins: Must remain unchanged
  • Writing style: Formal scientific language
  • Accurate spelling and grammar required

Files should be named as:
LastnameFirstname_paper.docx, LastnameFirstname_figures.ppt

Templates and Guide

  • NZJSJ Writing Reports Guide (PDF)
  • Core Concept Template (.docx)
  • Independent Investigation Template (.docx)
  • Figures Template (.ppt)
