Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who is allowed to submit?

New Zealand students in Years 7–8 and Years 9–10.

What types of papers may I submit?

Students can submit either a Core Concept paper or an Independent Investigation.

May I submit multiple papers?

No, only one paper per student is allowed.

Where can I find formatting guidelines and the appropriate template?

Visit our Guidelines page. At the bottom, you’ll find downloadable templates, the Writing Reports Guide, and a figure submission form.

Can I write a paper with someone else?

No, only individual submissions are accepted.

What citation/reference format should I use?

Use APA referencing, including both in-text citations and a bibliography.

Is resubmission allowed?

Yes, papers rejected in a previous issue may be submitted again.

Can I do simultaneous submissions?

Yes, but please notify us if your paper is accepted elsewhere.

How many papers do you publish?

Typically, four papers are published per issue.
